Cyber Security

Cyber security is the practice or collection of technologies, procedures, and practices designed to protect computer systems, networks, and data from cyber threats or attacks.

BIG DATA

Big data refers to large volumes of structured, semi-structured, and unstructured data that inundate a business on a day-to-day basis. It's not the amount of data that is significant, but what organizations do with the data that matters.

GAME TECHNOLOGY

Virtual reality (VR) refers to a computer-generated environment that simulates physical presence in real or imagined worlds. It allows users to interact with these environments and objects in a seemingly real way through sensory experiences, typically using special electronic equipment such as a VR headset.

GEOGRAPHICAL INFORMATION SYSTEM

A Geographical Information System (GIS) is a system designed to capture, store, analyze, manage, and present spatial or geographic data. It integrates hardware, software, and data for capturing, managing, analyzing, and displaying all forms of geographically referenced information.
